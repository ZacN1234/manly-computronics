To connect your domain manlycomputronics.com.au to your new website, we just need to update your nameservers. This is a simple change that takes about 5 minutes.

⚠️ Important: Email Safety

If you use email on this domain (e.g. help@manlycomputronics.com.au), I'll set up all your email records (MX, SPF, DKIM) on the new system before asking you to make this change. I won't ask you to switch until everything is ready and tested. Your email will not be interrupted.

Step-by-Step (Netregistry)

  1. 1 Log in to your Netregistry account at netregistry.com.au
  2. 2 Click "My Domains" or "Domain Names" in the dashboard
  3. 3 Click on "manlycomputronics.com.au"
  4. 4 Look for "Nameservers" or "DNS Settings" — click "Manage" or "Edit"
  5. 5 Replace the current nameservers with the new ones shown below
  6. 6 Click "Save" or "Update"
Current (remove these)
ns1.netregistry.net
ns2.netregistry.net
ns3.netregistry.net
New (enter these)
dave.ns.cloudflare.com
shubhi.ns.cloudflare.com

If there's a third nameserver field, leave it blank or remove it.

Timing: Changes usually take effect within 1 – 24 hours. During this time your site may show the old or new version — this is normal.

I'll Handle Everything Else

Once the nameservers are updated, I'll take care of: SSL certificate, DNS records, email configuration, and final testing. You don't need to do anything else.
