Frequently Asked Questions — Manly Computronics

Everything you need to know before you visit.

Where is Manly Computronics located?
We're at 119 Pittwater Road, Manly, NSW 2095 — near Manly Beach and a short walk from The Corso. Easy to find whether you're walking from the ferry, catching the bus, or driving in. There's metered street parking nearby.
What are your opening hours?
We're open Monday to Friday from 9am to 6pm, and Saturday from 9am to 2pm. We're closed on Sundays. Walk-ins are always welcome during business hours.
Do you repair Macs as well as PCs?
Absolutely. We repair both PCs and Macs — laptops and desktops. Whether it's a hardware issue, software problem, or you just need an upgrade, we've got you covered on both platforms.
How much does a computer repair cost?
It depends on the issue — a quick software fix costs less than a motherboard replacement, for example. We always tell you the price before we start — no surprises. Call us on (02) 9977 6433 or drop in for a free assessment.
Do you offer same-day repairs?
We do for many jobs, depending on the issue and whether we need to order parts. We know being without your computer is painful, so we don't sit on jobs.
Can you recover data from a crashed hard drive?
Yes — we handle data recovery from crashed, corrupted, and damaged drives. We also set up backup solutions so you're protected in the future. Bring the drive in and we'll let you know what's recoverable.
Do you remove viruses and malware?
We do. We remove viruses, malware, spyware, and adware, then secure your system so it's less likely to happen again. If your computer's running slow or showing pop-ups, that's usually a sign — bring it in.
Can you set up my home Wi-Fi network?
Yes, we set up and troubleshoot home and office Wi-Fi networks, broadband connections, and cabled networks. If your internet is slow or your Wi-Fi keeps dropping out, we can sort it.
Do you convert VHS tapes to digital?
We do — we convert VHS, 8mm, Super 8, and Betamax tapes to DVD. We also convert vinyl records to CD. It's a great way to preserve old family memories before the tapes degrade further.
Do you build custom PCs?
Yes, we build custom PCs tailored to your needs — whether it's for gaming, business, or a reliable home office machine. We also offer pre-purchase IT advice if you're thinking about buying something and want a second opinion.
Do you offer business IT support?
We do. We support a dozen local businesses — cafes, law firms, medical practices, holiday apartments — with server installation and maintenance, network setup, and day-to-day technical assistance. Give us a call to chat about how we can help your business.
What areas do you serve?
We're based in Manly and serve the entire Northern Beaches of Sydney, including Fairlight, Balgowlah, Queenscliff, North Manly, Manly Vale, Seaforth, Clontarf, Curl Curl, and the broader Warringah and Pittwater Peninsula areas.
